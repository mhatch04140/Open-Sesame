Manual Introduction

Open Sesame involves several componets working together. These components are an Andriod phone, an Android App, Bluetooth Technology, an Arduino, wire connections, and four servo motors. The Android phone host the Android app. The App connects to Arduino through blutooth technology. The Bluetooth singnal is received into the Arduino unit. The Arduino unit computes the command and activates the approiate servo motor.
