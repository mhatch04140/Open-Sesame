Manual Introduction

A number of components work together to make Open Sesame possible. The components include an Android phone, an Android app, Bluetooth technology, an Arduino, wire connections, and four servo motors. The Android app is hosted on the Android phone. Through Bluetooth technology, the App is able to connect to Arduino. Bluetooth signals are received by the Arduino unit. A servo motor is activated based on the command calculated by the Arduino unit.

The way Open Sesame works

Once the Open Sesame App is downloaded to the Android Phone, it is ready to use in voice recognition mode. Using Bluetooth, the voice recognition is transmitted to the Bluetooth module. It is the Bluetooth module that receives the signal and converts the voice into code that the Arduino can execute. To open or close the prototype's door, the Arduino sends signals to the appropriate servo motor.
